Brutal Online

STATUS: IN DEVELOPMENT  —  ENGINE: Godot 4  —  PLATFORM: PC

Overview

Free-to-play, PVP-focused, multiplayer ARPG. The Brutal is the ARPG game you've always wanted whether you knew it or not. 3 classes with hundreds of unique loot to customize and power your way to the top.

Gameplay

Enter the realm as one of three classes. Farm mobs for loot or venture into The Brutal to test your skill. Team up with your guild and raid dungeons for rare loot drop to get the edge.

Tools & Implementation

The Brutal Online is built in Godot 4, making use of Godot's many built-in networking tools. More info in devblogs

Status

Core networking architecture is complete. Currently, we are building and tuning the combat system and will begin playtesting soon.
