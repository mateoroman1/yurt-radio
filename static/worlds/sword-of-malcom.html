Sword of Malcom

STATUS: IN DEVELOPMENT  —  ENGINE: Godot 4  —  PLATFORM: PC

Overview

Singleplayer 3D Fantasy RPG that follows the story of Malachi Bricklayer, brother of Malcom. The sanctity of the world is in peril. Summoned at the behest of Mordar Bricklayer, you must retrieve the sword before its power is wielded by the forces of corruption and chaos.

Gameplay

Inspired by classics like Hexen and Kings Field, Sword of Malcom is a love-letter to the trail-blazing classics of the fantasy RPG genre. Hundreds of unique items, armor, and enemies to loot and fight on your mission to retrieve the sword.

Tools & Implementation

Sword of Malcom is built in Godot 4, using Trenchbroom for level design. Singleplayer, PC only.

Status

Development is briefly on pause to focus our efforts on Brutal Online.
